my 3 best movie all-time

Kabaddi Kabaddi (2015):

Directed by Ram Babu Gurung, this romantic comedy is a sequel to the 2013 film "Kabaddi." It follows the story of two strong-headed individuals and their unconventional love story. The film was well-received for its humor, performances, and depiction of contemporary Nepali society.

Pashupati Prasad (2016):

Directed by Dipendra K. Khanal, "Pashupati Prasad" is a social drama that revolves around the struggles of the protagonist, Prasad, as he tries to make a living in Kathmandu. The film addresses various social issues and received praise for its realistic portrayal of life in the city.

White Sun (Seto Surya) (2016):

Directed by Deepak Rauniyar, "White Sun" is a critically acclaimed drama set in the aftermath of the Nepalese civil war. The film explores themes of conflict, reconciliation, and societal changes. It received international recognition for its storytelling and cinematography.
